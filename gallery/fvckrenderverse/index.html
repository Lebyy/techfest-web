The gallery

Fvckrenderverse - 2021

Before LVCIDIA, there was the FVCKRENDERVERSE// — an immersive experience Fvckrender had created to showcase his digital artwork. Within that virtual environment, community member and filmmaker Ryan McCue saw a unique opportunity.

Ryan embarked on a journey to capture a striking photograph within the FVCKRENDERVERSE// every day for 100 days. The photographs were to be captured entirely in-game, using only the camera tool and its limited editing capabilities. Each photo was posted to the Discord daily as the community followed along. The final series, comprised of 100 photographs and 32 bonuses, reinforces the potential of immersive environments as new realms for artistic engagement and individual expression.
