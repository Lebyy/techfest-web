NFT NYC at YES - 2022

Following the major success of LVCIDIA at Studio 525 for NFT NYC, the venue was shut down by the NYPD and FDNY for the last night of NFT NYC. So on June 23rd 2022, the LVCIDIA event was moved to YES with a 3-hour turnaround, and all the collectors followed. YES was at full capacity to enjoy performances by TOKiMONSTA, and The Holy.
