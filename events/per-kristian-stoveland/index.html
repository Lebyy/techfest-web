Per Kristian Stoveland_ 09/12/2023

  • Artist of the Week

Initiating_Transmission: GM, esteemed collectors, visionaries of the digital realm, and explorers of the artistic cosmos. Today, we embark on a journey to the heart of Oslo, Norway, where the intersection of art, architecture, design, and technology forms a vibrant tapestry of creativity. Guiding us through this intricate landscape is Per Kristian Stoveland, a generative artist whose work transcends the boundaries of conventional art forms. Join us as we delve into his world, exploring his latest project, “The Harvest,” and uncovering the profound influences that have shaped his artistic identity.

Our Luminary: Per Kristian Stoveland

Per Kristian Stoveland, known as perkwerk, is a generative artist whose roots stretch from the cities and towns of Kenya and Zimbabwe to the bustling streets of Oslo. His childhood experiences in Africa, coupled with a Montessori education that emphasized sensory experience and creativity, laid the foundation for his artistic journey.

After graduating from The Oslo School of Graphic Design in 2003, Per Kristian’s path led him to co-found Void, an alternative design studio that stands at the crossroads of art and technology. His work at Void is a testament to his technical prowess, creative vision, and passion for generative art.

Shift

The Genesis of Generative Art

Per Kristian’s foray into generative art began with a seemingly small decision to build a website using Flash. This choice ignited a spark that propelled him into the endless landscape of coding and a perpetual hunger for learning. His experiments with generative art soon followed, culminating in a skill set that now breathes life into Void’s installations and his personal projects.

His recent reconnection with the blockchain and NFTs has further fueled his creative fire. The blockchain’s synergy with generative art has rekindled his passion, leading to the creation of “The Harvest.”

The Harvest #112

The Harvest

“The Harvest” is a project where Per Kristian’s deep fascination for physics, space, the cosmos, and science fiction meets his art practice for the first time. Inspired by his love for storytelling and world-building, this project explores the worlds visited by an entity called “the caretaker.”

The algorithm behind “The Harvest” creates landscapes that feel familiar yet possess an alien vibe. The beams that decorate the landscape tell a story of harvesting units sent out to different worlds, resulting in stunning color mixing and visual effects.

Collectors can look forward to unique compositions, sci-fi references, and a turning point in Per Kristian’s artistic identity. “The Harvest” represents a moment of self-reflection and a newfound creative direction.



The Harvest #274

Void

Void’s recent projects, such as Delta and C8 – Tesseract, showcase Per Kristian’s ability to play with perception and dimensionality. These permanent installations, embedded with light sculptures and intricate designs, offer a mesmerizing experience that transcends traditional art forms.

Delta, a permanent, embedded light sculpture in Oslo, took three years to complete and represents a significant accomplishment for Void. The installation’s interaction with pedestrians and its integration with live music during the grand opening demonstrates the studio’s innovative approach.

C8 - Tesseract

A Journey Within

Per Kristian’s creative process is a blend of inspiration, experimentation, and coding. His ability to design while coding reflects a streamlined and integrated approach that has evolved over twenty years.

His insights on self-doubt, creativity, and success offer a glimpse into the mind of an artist who has learned to overcome obstacles and embrace the unknown. His advice to aspiring artists? “Don’t overthink, don’t wait, just start making art.”

We Are Alone Together

Art as a Living Entity

Per Kristian Stoveland’s work is a living testament to the power of curiosity, learning, and artistic expression. His projects, whether at Void or in his generative art practice, resonate with a sense of wonder and exploration.

His art is not just a visual spectacle; it’s a sensory experience that invites us to ponder the universe, the cosmos, and our place within it. It’s a celebration of creativity that challenges our perceptions and invites us to see the world through a different lens.

Colonies #6

Behind the Keys

Per Kristian’s journey as an artist is marked by a constant evolution and a willingness to embrace new challenges. From his early days in Africa to his current work with Void and generative art, his story is one of growth, exploration, and innovation.

His perspective on success, failure, creativity, and the role of art in his life offers valuable insights for anyone seeking to understand the mind of a creative visionary. Whether discussing his favorite failure or the books that have influenced him, Per Kristian’s reflections provide a window into his world.

Blaatimen

A Legacy of Innovation

As we conclude this exploration, let us carry with us the inspiration and wisdom that Per Kristian has shared. May it ignite a spark in our own creative journeys and lead us to new horizons.

His work, from the intricate designs of “The Harvest” to the groundbreaking installations of Void, stands as a testament to the power of creativity, innovation, and artistic vision. Per Kristian Stoveland’s legacy is one of curiosity, courage, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Until our next artistic adventure, continue seeking, understanding, and exploring.

Bonglo

His journey from coastal Australia to being a published Environmental Microbiologist at Texas A&M has instilled in him a rigorous, data-driven approach. This methodology, shaped by curiosity & the scientific method, is now applied to his work collaborating with artists in the web3 space.
