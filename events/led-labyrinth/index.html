Welcome to the LED Labyrinth – where darkness meets illumination, and every twist and turn leads you deeper into an electrifying adventure! Prepare to embark on a journey unlike any other as you and your team dive headfirst into the maze of lights and mysteries.

What is LED Labyrinth?

You and your team of five are locked into a room filled with electrifying puzzles. Your mission spans different stages, each packed with tricky challenges. To progress, you must crack codes, connect circuits, and unravel the room's secret code to escape. It's like stepping into a real-life video game, surrounded by gadgets, wires, and mysterious contraptions. Every solved puzzle unlocks the key to the next level, bringing you closer to freedom. But here's the catch – time is ticking away!

Event details

Date : March 6, 7, 9

Time :10 am to 5 pm

Venue : VIT Basement,Lab 16

Registration Dates : [dates]

Entry fees : No entry fee!

Prizes :

  • Winning team will get a cash prize of 2500 Rupees along with Trophy.
  • Runner Up Team will get a cash prize of RS 1000 along with Trophy.

Event Flow

March 6th & 7th

10:00 AM - Event Start

Welcome and introduction to the LED Labyrinth. Brief overview of the rules, schedule, and scoring system.

10:15 AM to 12:00 PM - Escape

Escape Room begins with each team having 10 minutes to solve 4 stages of difficulties. Teams get only one attempt, and their time gets recorded.

12:00 PM to 01:00 PM - Break (1 hour)

Participants take a break for lunch and rest.

01:00 PM to 05:00 PM - Escape

The match begins with each winning team scoring +10 points for their team. Teams continue to compete in a series of matches.
